Introduction To Demon Slayer!

The story follows a young man named Tanjiro whose mountain-dwelling family are slaughtered by demons and his sister Nezuko has been transformed into a demon herself. Looking to restore his sister’s humanity, Tanjiro studies under a demon slayer to become one himself, before setting out on a demon hunt across Japan.
